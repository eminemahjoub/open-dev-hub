# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct (v2.1)

We expect contributors to behave professionally and respectfully. This project follows the Contributor Covenant v2.1.

Examples of expected behavior:
- Be respectful and considerate in language and tone.
- Give constructive, kind feedback.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.

Unacceptable behavior includes:
- Harassment, threats, or hate speech.
- Disrespectful or derogatory comments.
- Personal attacks or doxxing.

Report issues to the maintainers (add contact email or open an issue flagged for maintainers). All reports will be handled promptly and respectfully.

For full text and details, see https://www.contributor-covenant.org
